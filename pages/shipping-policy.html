Returns Policy

Cruising Through the Guidelines:
At Torque & Texture, we're passionate about turning your automotive dreams into wall-worthy decor. But sometimes, even the smoothest roads have unexpected turns. That's why we've built a hassle-free return policy to ensure your journey with us is a joyride from start to finish.

Time Trials:
You have 30 days from the date of delivery to return your print for a full refund or exchange. No need for pit stops, just let us know your intention to return within this timeframe.

Condition Checkup:
To keep the engine purring, we ask that your returned print be in pristine condition, just like it rolled off the assembly line.
This means:
Unused and undamaged: No tire marks, coffee stains, or unauthorized paint jobs!
Original packaging: Securely pack the print back in its original box and protective materials.
Proof of purchase: Show us your receipt or order number so we can verify your pit stop.
Taking the Fast Lane:
For exchanges, simply let us know the new print you'd like to swap for in your return request. We'll handle the rest like a well-oiled machine.

Change of Heart:
While we're confident you'll love your print, things might not always click like perfectly-aligned gears. For change-of-mind returns, kindly note that shipping costs will be deducted from your refund.

Exceptions to the Rule:
Just like every car has its blind spots, there are a few exceptions to our return policy:
Custom prints: These beauties are one-of-a-kind creations, so returns are not possible unless they arrive damaged. Discounted items: Clearance prints are final sale, but let us know if they arrive in less-than-stellar condition.

Navigating the Process:
Ready to return your print? No need to call a tow truck! Just follow these steps:
Contact our friendly customer service team through [+94 75 113 4064/torquetexture@gmail.com]. Request a Return Authorization Number (RAN).
Securely pack your print as described earlier, including the RAN inside the package.
Ship the package back to us using a trackable service (your choice).
Sit back and relax! We'll process your return within 7 business days of receiving the print.
